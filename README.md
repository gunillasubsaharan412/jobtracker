## 🚀 How to use the tracker

The application organizes your workflow into four main categories. You can view, add, or edit these items at any time.

### Viewing your dashboard
When you open the software, the main screen displays your active applications. Each row shows the company name, the job title, and the current status. Status labels include Applied, Interview Scheduled, and Offer Received.

### Adding a new job
Click the plus sign button at the top corner of the screen. A window appears. Enter the job title and the company name. Provide the URL for the job posting if you have it. Choose a status from the drop-down menu. Press save to add this item to your main list.

### Updating your progress
Click on any job in your list. The edit window opens. You can update the status as you move through each stage. You can also add notes about the recruiter or specific details about the role. Save your changes to see the new status on your dashboard.

### Removing items
Sometimes you may choose to remove an entry. Right-click the job in your list. A menu appears with a delete option. Confirm your choice to remove the item from your dashboard.

## 🔍 Frequently asked questions

### Will the software work if I go offline?
Yes, the application stores your information on your local computer. You do not need a constant connection to view your lists.

### Can I export my data?
You can export your list into a generic file format for use in other programs. Go to the file menu and select Export. Choose a location on your computer and save the file.

### How do I update the application?
When a newer version becomes available, the program informs you upon launch. Click the update link in the notification window to download the latest setup file. Running this file updates your app without erasing your existing information.

### What happens if I lose my data?
The software saves your information automatically. We suggest that you save a backup of your data folder to an external drive or cloud storage once a week to protect against computer hardware failure.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting tips

If you encounter issues, these steps fix most common problems.

* If the program does not open, restart your computer. This clears stuck processes from your system memory.
* If your window appears blank, resize the window by dragging the corner. This forces the display to redraw your content.
* If you cannot find your file, check your Downloads folder. Windows places most files there by default.
* If the screen looks too small, go to the settings menu. You can change your zoom level to improve readability.
* Should the application crash, locate the log file in the install folder. The text inside the file describes what caused the event.
